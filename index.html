Roll It

The Roll It game is a simple dice game where players take turns rolling a die. The goal is to be the first player to reach 50 points.

How to Play:

  • On your turn, you can roll the die as many times as you want.
  • If you roll a 1, you lose all points for that turn and your total score resets to zero.
  • If you roll any other number, you can choose to either roll again or hold your score.
  • If you hold, your current score is added to your total score.
  • The first player to reach 50 points wins!
